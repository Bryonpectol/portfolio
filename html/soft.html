  • Spanish:
  • In 2018, I went on a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. I served in Guayaquil, Ecuador. Before I could go down to Ecuador to serve, I had to learn Spanish in the MTC (Missionary Training Center). I spent 6 weeks there, and I would spend 6-8 hours a day learning how to speak Spanish. After I finished the MTC, I flew out to Ecuador to teach the people there. I was supposed to stay in Ecuador for 2 years, but ended up coming back to the United States early. I came home early because, I got Myocarditis, which is an infection in your heart. I was forced to come home. I ended up serving for 4 months in Ecuador before I had to return home. However, I still learned how to speak Spanish fluently before I had to come home. Now that I am home, I speak Spanish every day at my current job.
